The Best Movies of All Times According to Geraldine.

My Top 3 Favorite Movies of All Time.

1. The Vampire Diaries (2009-2017)

The Vampire Diaries is a supernatural drama television series based on the book series of the same name by L. J. Smith. The show follows the life of Elena Gilbert, a teenage girl who falls in love with a vampire named Stefan Salvatore.
The series explores themes of love, friendship, and the supernatural, and has gained a large following for its compelling characters and engaging storyline.
The show has received critical acclaim for its writing, acting, and production values, and has been praised for its ability to balance romance, drama, and supernatural elements.
Overall, The Vampire Diaries is a captivating and entertaining series that has left a lasting impact on its fans and the television industry as a whole.
Favorite Character: Nicklaus Mikaelson (played by Joseph Morgan)

2. The Big Bang Theory (2007-2019)

The Big Bang Theory is a popular sitcom that revolves around a group of socially awkward scientists and their interactions with each other and the world around them. The show follows the lives of Leonard, Sheldon, Howard, Raj, and their friends as they navigate through their personal and professional lives.
The series is known for its witty humor, clever writing, and memorable characters. It has received critical acclaim for its portrayal of geek culture and its ability to blend comedy with heartfelt moments.
Overall, The Big Bang Theory is a beloved sitcom that has entertained audiences for over a decade and has left a lasting impact on popular culture.
Favorite Character: Sheldon Cooper (played by Jim Parsons)

3. King of Boys (2018-2020)

King of Boys is a Nigerian political thriller film directed by Kemi Adetiba.
The movie follows the story of Alhaja Eniola Salami, a powerful businesswoman and politician who finds herself in a dangerous power struggle after being betrayed by her closest allies.
The film explores themes of power, corruption, and loyalty, and has been praised for its strong performances, gripping storyline, and social commentary on Nigerian politics.
Overall, King of boys is a compelling and thought-provoking film that has garnered critical acclaim and has become a must-watch for fans of political thrillers and Nigerian cinema.
Best to watch with Family and Friends.
Favorite Character: Alhaja Eniola Salami (played by Sola Sobowale)
